# Script Acts 1-3

## Act 1 · Archive Entry

- player enters the reconstruction space
- learns the archive match loop
- receives first broken contact from Dr. Vance
- tutorial puzzle establishes evidence correlation

## Act 2 · Site-13 Descent

- stone golem becomes a persistent threat
- archive fragments reveal the failed experiment
- director pressure escalates through audiovisual corruption
- second major puzzle proves the archive is changing the present

## Act 3 · Echo Collapse

- truth about the site and Marcus converges
- player chooses whether to preserve, expose, or sever the echo
- final confrontation is more about control and revelation than combat
