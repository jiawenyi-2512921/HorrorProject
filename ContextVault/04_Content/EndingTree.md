# Ending Tree

| Ending | Condition | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| Preserve the Echo | player chooses containment over truth | tragic, unresolved, preservation through denial |
| Expose the Site | player favors truth and release | cathartic, dangerous, revelatory |
| Sever the Channel | player rejects both archive and echo continuity | bleak, clean, final |

## Requirement

All endings must feel like consequences of what the player learned, not arbitrary switches.
