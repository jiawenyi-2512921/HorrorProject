# Puzzles

| Puzzle | Core action | Failure feedback | Reveal threshold |
|---|---|---|---|
| Timestamp Match | align archive evidence to the correct incident window | incorrect archive produces false reconstruction | after repeated misses, highlight relevant date range |
| Statue Array | place stone forms by recovered research logic | wrong arrangement spikes intensity and misdirects golem | after three failures, unlock partial clue |
| Instrument Restart | restore a dead lab chain in the right order | wrong order corrupts signal and delays NPC clarity | after repeated failure, reveal next valid subsystem |

## Rule

No puzzle may hard-lock narrative progression without an assist path.
