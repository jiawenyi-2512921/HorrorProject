# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 26, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Horror Project ("we", "our", or "the game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play our game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data

With your explicit consent, we collect the following analytics data:

**Performance Metrics:**
- Frames per second (FPS)
- Frame rendering time
- Memory usage
- Application performance data

**Gameplay Metrics:**
- Game progress and level completion
- In-game achievements
- Gameplay duration and session length
- In-game actions and events

**Technical Information:**
- Device platform (Windows, Mac, etc.)
- Application version
- Error logs and crash reports

### What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Payment information
- Location data
- Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)
- Biometric data
- Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for:

1. **Game Improvement:** Analyzing gameplay patterns to improve game design and balance
2. **Performance Optimization:** Identifying and fixing performance issues
3. **Bug Fixing:** Detecting and resolving technical issues
4. **Feature Development:** Understanding which features players enjoy most

We do NOT use your data for:
- Advertising or marketing
- Selling to third parties
- Profiling or automated decision-making
- Any purpose other than game improvement

## Data Anonymization

All user identifiers are anonymized using cryptographic hashing (MD5) before storage. This means:

- Your actual user ID is never stored
- Data cannot be traced back to you personally
- Anonymized IDs cannot be reversed to reveal your identity

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

### Right to Access
You can request a copy of all data we have collected about you.

**How to exercise:**
```
Settings → Privacy → Export My Data
```

### Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You can request deletion of all your data at any time.

**How to exercise:**
```
Settings → Privacy → Delete My Data
```

### Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw your consent for analytics collection at any time.

**How to exercise:**
```
Settings → Privacy → Analytics Settings → Disable Analytics
```

### Right to Data Portability
You can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON).

**How to exercise:**
```
Settings → Privacy → Export My Data
```

### Right to Object
You can object to data processing by disabling analytics.

**How to exercise:**
```
Settings → Privacy → Analytics Settings → Disable Analytics
```

## Your Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

### Right to Know
You have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it.

### Right to Delete
You have the right to request deletion of your personal information.

### Right to Opt-Out
You have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of personal information. **Note:** We do not sell personal information.

### Right to Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

### How to Exercise Your Rights

```

```

## Consent Management

### Initial Consent

When you first launch the game, you will be asked to provide consent for:

- **Analytics Collection:** General gameplay and performance analytics
- **Crash Reporting:** Automatic crash report submission
- **Personalized Experience:** (Optional) Personalized game recommendations

You can choose to:
- Accept all
- Accept only essential (crash reporting)
- Decline all (analytics disabled)

### Changing Consent

You can change your consent preferences at any time:

```
Settings → Privacy → Manage Consent
```

Changes take effect immediately.

### Consent for Minors

If you are under 16 years old (or under 13 in the US), parental consent is required for analytics collection.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All analytics data is stored locally on your device in:
```
[Game Installation]/Saved/Analytics/
```

### Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- **Encryption:** Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
- **Access Control:** Analytics data is only accessible by the game
- **Anonymization:** User IDs are anonymized before storage
- **No Network Transmission:** Data is not transmitted over the network (unless you explicitly export it)

### Data Retention

- **Active Data:** Retained while the game is installed
- **Exported Data:** Retained until you delete it
- **Deleted Data:** Permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request

## Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:
- Third-party advertisers
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Any other third parties

**Exception:** We may share anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., "50% of players completed Level 1") for:
- Public statistics
- Press releases
- Research purposes

This aggregated data cannot be used to identify individual users.

## Cookies and Tracking

This game does NOT use:
- Cookies
- Web beacons
- Tracking pixels
- Third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics)

All analytics are collected and processed locally.
